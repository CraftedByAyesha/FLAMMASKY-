OUR BEGINNING

A Passion Forged in Flame

FLAMMA SKY was founded in 2018 with a vision to bring together exceptional food, warm hospitality, and unforgettable ambience.

Inspired by the art of fire-grilled cooking, our chefs transform fresh ingredients into bold and memorable flavors that delight every guest.

From intimate indoor dining to our open-air terrace beneath the sky, every corner of FLAMMA SKY is designed to create moments worth sharing.

Today, we continue to celebrate food, family, and community—one perfectly crafted meal at a time.

OUR JOURNEY

From a Dream to a Landmark

2018

The First Spark

FLAMMA SKY opens its doors in Hyderabad with a small menu and a big vision for honest, fire-cooked food.

2020

Glowing the Flame

We add our signature outdoor BBQ pit and expand the menu to 30+ items, welcoming our first 500 loyal guests.

2022

Hyderabad's Favourite

FLAMMA SKY is voted one of the city's top BBQ restaurants with a 4.8-star guest rating and a growing reputation.

2024

Innovation of the Menu

New menu additions, redesigned dining hall, and the same soul — FLAMMA SKY continues to grow, one great meal at a time.

2026

The Future Beckons

Expanded our digital presence,refined our offerings,and continued creating unforgettable moments for every guest.
